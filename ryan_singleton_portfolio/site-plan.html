Overview

Purpose

Have you ever had an idea for a website, web app, or software that could help make the world a better place, but you weren't sure how to do it? That's where I come in. This website demonstrates some of my recent work and displays some of my technical skills in a very visual and digestible way. I can help bring your vision to life by implementing your idea into something tangible and usable. I am confident that this work will help to foster trust in me as a future partner and I am happy to answer in questions you may have.

Audience

My intended audience is primarily going to be potential clients or employers. They will be looking to gain proof of my ability to help them, and this site will foster trust. My audience is more specifically and most likely going to be technical recruiters that are looking for candidates that stand out in their ability to showcase their skillset.

To-Do App: This is a To-Do app built with HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Bootstrap columns are leveraged for styling and responsiveness. The app accepts user inputs for the dates the task starts and ends, as well as the task itself.
