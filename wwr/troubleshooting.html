Overview

Purposes

The purpose of this site is to communicate with its visitors the fun that they can have by using Raging River Rafting's services to go white water rafting. It is to convey to the visitors that they can create lasting memories, and those memories can be associated with Raging River Rapids, and promote repeat package purchases.

Audience

My target audience mostly includes lovers of the outdoors. These people can range from ages 12-70 (possibly higher), but the typical adventurous crowd would probably fall between the ages of 16-30. These people probably also enjoy camping and hiking. Groups most likely include family units, or friend groups of similar ages. This audience is looking for a thrill. They are looking for a fun time with their friends and/or family. The main need that isn't being met is stress free fun. People have a lot of reasons to worry, and this audience may be seeking a way to alleviate some of that stress for a while, as well as connect with those that they white water raft with through teamwork. This audience will most likely access my site via mobile device, but laptop and desktop will also need to be accounted for.
